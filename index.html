INTERESTING FACTS

1. Leather as a breathable material: Natural leather has an amazing ability to allow air to pass through, making it comfortable in any season

2. Automotive velour: This material is not only pleasant to the touch but also highly durable, making it ideal for car interiors

3. Vinyl as a versatile material: It can imitate leather, is waterproof, and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice for upholstering furniture and cars

4. Stain-resistant fabrics: Modern upholstery fabrics are often treated with special coatings that protect against stains and make cleaning easier

5. A second life for furniture: Upholstery not only restores the original look of furniture or car interiors but can completely change their style, creating the feeling of a brand-new item

6. Weather resistance: Modern materials for boat and car upholstery include UV-resistant fabrics that protect against fading and sun damage

 7. Custom design: Upholstery allows for creating unique designs by adding non-standard stitching, embroidery, and combinations of different materials and colors

8. Increased property value: High-quality restoration of car interiors or antique furniture can significantly boost their market value

9. Durability and longevity: Modern upholstery materials, such as microfiber and automotive vinyl, can last longer than the original coverings

10. Soundproofing: In some cases, replacing materials in a car interior can improve sound insulation, making drives more comfortable

11. Room for experimentation: Upholstery trends include eco-friendly materials, unusual textures (such as suede or carbon), and advanced technologies like built-in heating elements
